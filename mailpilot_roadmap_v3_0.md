# MailPilot v3.0: The Autonomous Ecosystem

*The final evolution: An autonomous agent that lives in your OS and handles your entire professional digital life.*

## 1. Full Agentic Autonomy
*   **Independent Action:** James can be given permission to "Auto-Handle" low-priority emails (e.g., "Schedule a haircut" or "Confirm the invoice").
*   **Approval Queue:** A high-speed interface where you swipe left/right to approve actions James wants to take.

## 2. Voice & Wearable Integration
*   **Talk to James:** Voice-to-text integration for road warriors. *"James, what's my first priority today?"*
*   **Smartwatch Notifications:** Contextual "Action Cards" delivered to Apple Watch/Android Wear.

## 3. The Extension Ecosystem
*   **Notion/Slack/Asana Sync:** Seamlessly offloading tasks to your existing company tools.
*   **MailPilot API:** Allowing other developers to build "James Plugins" (e.g., a "Legal Plugin" for summarizing law documents).

## 4. Enterprise-Grade Privacy (MailPilot Pro)
*   **Local Processing:** Option to run smaller models (like Nemotron-Mini) entirely on-device for maximum privacy.
*   **Audit Logs:** Full traceability of every AI decision and action.
