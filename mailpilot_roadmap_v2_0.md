# MailPilot v2.0: The Proactive Bureau Edition

*Transforming MailPilot from a summary tool into a proactive executive assistant that handles your schedule and documents automatically.*

## 1. "Magic" Calendar Integration (The Splash Feature)
*   **Availability Awareness:** When an email asks for a meeting, James automatically checks your Google Calendar.
*   **One-Click Confirmation:** A green "You're Free" button appears on the dashboard, allowing you to confirm a meeting and send a reply in a single click.
*   **Conflict Warnings:** If you're busy, James suggests the next best available time.

## 2. Proactive Drafting (The "Morning Brief")
*   **Automated Pre-Drafting:** James drafts replies to all "ACTION_REQUIRED" emails before you even open the dashboard.
*   **Executive Recap:** A visual "Morning Brief" page summarizing what happened while you were asleep.

## 3. Deep-Dive Attachment Intelligence
*   **Document OCR:** James reads attached PDFs, Word Docs, and Resumes.
*   **Holistic Extraction:** AI extracts tasks from *inside* attachments (e.g., "Review Clause 4 in the attached contract").

## 4. The James Command Bar
*   **Natural Language Actions:** A "CMD+K" bar to talk to James: *"James, find that resume from yesterday"* or *"James, tell everyone I'm running 5 mins late."*
*   **Global Search:** Searching across emails, tasks, and calendar events.

## 5. Visual Productivity Analytics
*   **Time-Saved Counter:** Visualizing the hours saved by James.
*   **Communication Heatmap:** Insights into your busiest times and top contacts.

## 6. Smart "Fast Pass" Optimization
*   **Single-Trip Analysis:** For shorter emails, James handles summarization and polishing in a single AI call, nearly doubling sync speed.
## 7. Persistent Infrastructure Upgrade
*   **Managed Database Migration:** Transition from local SQLite to a managed Postgres provider (Supabase/Neon).
*   **Zero-Loss Persistence:** Ensuring all tasks, AI personas, and training feedback are 100% persistent across sessions and logout cycles.
*   **Encrypted Secrets Management:** Moving all API keys and secrets to a dedicated managed vault.
